  # Engineering Principles

Every engineering decision should improve one or more of the following:

- Developer Productivity
- Reliability
- Maintainability
- Scalability
- Observability
- Reusability

If it doesn't improve one of these, we question why we're building it.

---

## Build Once. Reuse Forever.

Frameworks are preferred over duplicated implementations.

---

## Document Decisions

Important decisions are documented.

---

## Ship Continuously

Small, consistent progress compounds.

---

## Build in Public

Share learnings, architecture, and ideas while protecting employer and customer confidentiality.
